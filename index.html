Monday, September 29 – Sunday, October 5, 2025

In a city built around cars, it can be nearly impossible - or even dangerous - to travel without one. For the one-third of people in the U.S. who can’t drive due to age, income, ability, or other circumstances, every trip is a challenge. Week Without Driving offers a powerful opportunity for residents and policymakers to experience these challenges firsthand and reflect on what a truly inclusive, multi-modal transportation system could look like in North Texas.
