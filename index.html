Monday, September 29 – Sunday, October 5, 2025

Approximately one-third of people in the United States cannot drive due to age, ability, income, and other factors. Every community includes people who don’t drive.

Week Without Driving invites everyone to experience, reflect, and share what life is like without driving. It’s an opportunity to tell your story and help reimagine what transportation options could look like in your community. See more about the national campaign: weekwithoutdriving.org
