Dallas Bicycle Coalition

The Dallas Bicycle Coalition brings together cycling advocates to promote safer streets, improved infrastructure, and a thriving cycling culture. Partnering with local groups and businesses, it supports advocacy, education, and community events, aiming for a future where cycling is safe, popular, and accessible.

Better Block

Better Block revitalizes underused spaces through a 120-day design process focused on people over cars. With 10 projects a year and a mobile "Better Block in a Box" kit, they foster community, reduce crime, and support ESG goals through sustainable urban design.

Dallas Urbanists STLC

Dallas Urbanists is a Strong Towns Location Conversation. We connect like-minded residents to promote informed, empathetic conversations on walkability, transportation, and housing.

Dallas Area Transit Alliance / DATA

The Dallas Area Transit Alliance (DATA) advocates for stronger public transit across all 13 DART cities. Founded in 2024 to oppose DART funding cuts, DATA works to protect service, influence policy, and promote practical transit solutions.

Dallas Neighbors for Housing

Dallas Neighbors for Housing is a grassroots chapter of YIMBYAction advocating for more affordable, equitable housing in Dallas — housing close to jobs, transit, parks, and food.

Friends of Northaven Trail

Friends of Northaven Trail is an all-volunteer organization supporting the maintenance, beautification, and expansion of the nine-mile Northaven Trail for recreation, commuting, native habitat restoration, and connectivity in Dallas.

Environment Texas

Environment Texas is a statewide advocacy group working to protect open spaces, clean air and water, wildlife, and promote clean energy — pushing for greener, healthier policies through research, education, and legal action.

Dallas Housing Coalition

Dallas Housing Coalition is a coalition of over 350 organizations and individuals advocating for increased public investment in housing, preserving affordable homes, and ensuring attainable housing across income levels in the Dallas region.

Friends of Bachman Lake

Friends of Bachman Lake is a nonprofit advocating for a clean, safe & connected Bachman Lake Park — improving trails, park amenities, and green space access in a long-neglected part of Northwest Dallas.
